Introduction

IBM (International Business Machines Corporation) is a global technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York, United States. It is known for its computer hardware, software, and services.

History

IBM was founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR) and was renamed to IBM in 1924. It played a major role in the development of early computers and remains a major player in IT innovation.
